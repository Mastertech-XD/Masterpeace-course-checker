About Us

Welcome to the KUCCPS Qualification Checker – your ultimate tool for verifying academic eligibility for Kenyan university and college programs.

Choosing the right career path is one of the most important decisions in a student's life. Our goal is to simplify this process by providing an easy-to-use platform where students can check their qualifications for various courses offered through KUCCPS. Whether you’re aiming for a Degree, Diploma, Certificate, or KMTC program, we’ve got you covered.

Our system analyzes your academic performance, calculates cluster points, and matches them with the latest KUCCPS requirements. This ensures that you make well-informed decisions based on accurate data.
